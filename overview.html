Wangle: a Tool for Literate Programming
Overview

The technique of Literate Programming was first proposed by Prof. Donald Knuth way back in 1984. He described a literate program as a web of interconnected fragments from which one could tangle pieces together to make a compilable program, or weave them together to make the documentation.
